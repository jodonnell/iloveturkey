I owned a Turkey Once

His name was sugar lips. I thought that was funny because turkeys don't have lips. They have something so much more sensual. Sugar lips would gaze at me longingly while I cooked. Sometimes I would look back and our eyes would meet, just for a second. I had sugar lips for three days before he got cancer. He went bald and had a huge tumor on his side. The attraction was lost and sugar lips died alone.
